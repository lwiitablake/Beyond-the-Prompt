WORKFLOW

Responsible AI Workflow

A repeatable process for making instructional decisions about AI.

Use this workflow when revising a page, creating examples, drafting materials, reviewing AI output, or deciding whether AI belongs in a task at all.

Why this workflow scales

What should you do? Use the same decision process across tools, disciplines, and learning contexts.

Why is this better for learners? A stable process helps keep privacy, accessibility, equity, and learning at the center even when AI tools change.

The seven steps

Step 1

Identify the instructional problem

What are you trying to improve?

Step 2

Decide if AI is appropriate

Should AI help with this task?

Step 3

Gather context

What information does the work need?

Step 4

Generate a draft

How can AI create a starting point?

Step 5

Use human review and judgment

What must you check before using it?

Step 6

Ask if it better supports learners

Does this improve the learning experience?

Step 7

Reflect and improve

What should change next time?
