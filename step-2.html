WORKFLOW STEP 2

Decide if AI is appropriate

Should AI help with this task?

What should you do?

Pause before using AI. Check whether the task is safe, useful, and appropriate for AI support.

Why is this better for learners?

Choosing when not to use AI protects privacy, preserves human judgment, and keeps learning from being outsourced.

Things to check

Decision Check

Does it involve private information?

If it includes names, grades, accommodations, messages, or submissions, do not use a public AI tool.

Use an approved tool or remove private information.

Decision Check

Does it affect a high-stakes decision?

If it affects grades, eligibility, discipline, or access, keep human judgment central.

Check policy before continuing.

Try it

Write “AI is appropriate here because…” or “AI is not appropriate here because…”.
