INTERACTIVE DECISION TREE

Find the right next step

Use this page when you are not sure where to begin.

Open the branch closest to your task. Each branch asks a few questions and points you toward a next step.

Before you start

What should you do? Start with the task, not the tool.

Why is this better for learners? Starting with the task keeps AI use focused on learning, access, privacy, and support.

Choose your starting point

Open the branch closest to what you are trying to do. Keep the rest closed unless you need them.

Branch 1 I want to use AI for a course task Should AI be involved at all? 
flowchart TD
    A(["Start: I want to use AI"]) --> B{"Does this include private learner information?"}
    B -->|Yes| C["Stop: Do not use a public AI tool"]
    B -->|No| D{"Does this affect grades,eligibility, accommodations, discipline, or access?"}
    D -->|Yes| E["Check policy;Keep human judgment central"]
    D -->|No| F{"Would AI replace the skill learners need to practice?"}
    F -->|Yes| G["Do not use AI this way Redesign the task"]
    F -->|No| H["AI may be appropriate. Use it for brainstorming, drafting, organizing, or revision"]
    H --> I["Next: Use the Decision Card Should AI be used here?"]

Branch 2 I want to upload something Is it safe to share with an AI tool? 
flowchart TD
    A([Start: I want to upload something]) --> B{Does it include learner names, grades, IDs, messages, accommodations, or submissions?}
    B -->|Yes| C[Stop: Do not upload it to a public AI tool]
    B -->|No| D{Does it include private institutional information?}
    D -->|Yes| E[Stop: Use an approved institutional process]
    D -->|No| F{Does it include copyrighted or licensed material?}
    F -->|Yes| G[Check the license or use a safer excerpt]
    F -->|No| H[You may continue with human review]
    G --> I[Next: Use the Decision Card called Can I upload this?]
    H --> I

Next step

Open the Cards page and use “Can I upload this?”

Branch 3 I want to improve course materials Will this better support learners? 
flowchart TD
    A([Start: I want to improve course materials]) --> B{Can learners tell what to do first?}
    B -->|No| C[Revise the structure and directions]
    B -->|Yes| D{Are links, files, images, tables, and media accessible?}
    D -->|No| E[Fix access barriers before sharing]
    D -->|Yes| F{Does this support the learning goal?}
    F -->|No| G[Revise or remove it]
    F -->|Yes| H{Does it reduce confusion or create more of it?}
    H -->|Creates confusion| I[Revise for clarity]
    H -->|Reduces confusion| J[Ready for final human review]
    J --> K[Next: Use the Decision Card called Is this ready for learners?]

Next step

Open the Cards page and use “Is this ready for learners?”

Branch 4 I want learners to use AI Does AI support or replace the learning? 
flowchart TD
    A([Start: I want learners to use AI]) --> B{What skill are learners supposed to practice?}
    B --> C{Would AI complete that skill for them?}
    C -->|Yes| D[Restrict AI or redesign the task]
    C -->|No| E{Does AI support brainstorming, revision, practice, or reflection?}
    E -->|Yes| F{Are expectations clear?}
    E -->|No| G[Do not include AI unless it supports the learning goal]
    F -->|No| H[Explain what is allowed, what must be disclosed, and what learners must do themselves]
    F -->|Yes| I[AI use may be appropriate]
    H --> J[Next: Use the Decision Card called Should learners use AI for this?]
    I --> J
