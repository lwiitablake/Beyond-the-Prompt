WORKFLOW STEP 5

Use human review and judgment

What must you check before using it?

What should you do?

Review the draft for accuracy, accessibility, privacy, alignment, plain language, inclusion, and learner support.

Why is this better for learners?

Human review helps prevent confident errors, hidden barriers, stereotypes, and confusing instructions from reaching learners.

Review dimensions

Accuracy

Check facts, examples, links, and citations.

Accessibility

Check headings, links, images, media, tables, and reading order.

Learner impact

Check whether the tone, examples, and options support different learners.

Try it

Choose one AI output and mark it: use, revise, reject, or ask for another draft.
